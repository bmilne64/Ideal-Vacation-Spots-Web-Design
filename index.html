Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to show the weather conditions of a random set of places in the world, and narrowed those conditions to specific preferences to determine the ideal places to vacation.Â 


First I created a Python script that randomly selected at least 500 unique (non-repeated) cities based on latitude and longitude. Second, I performed a weather check on each of the cities using a series of successive API calls. Finally, I created a series of scatter plots to showcase the following relationships: Temperature (F) vs. Latitude, Humidity (%) vs. Latitude, Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude and Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude.
